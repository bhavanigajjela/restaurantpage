About our Restaurant..

kritunga restaurantwas established in the year 2002 to bring flavours of Rayalaseema cuisine to food lovers in Hyderabad. A menu of dishes curated by dedicated chefs and staff, today remains to be one of the favourite brands in restaurant chains, in South India.
Kritunga aims to serve authentic Rayalseema fare in comfortable surroundings and at prices that do not burn the pockets of the customers. Our concept combines qualityfood, ambiance and a superior staff to create a sense of ‘belonging’ in order to reach our goal to give a holistic value to the dining experience. We offer fair profits for the franchise owners and investors, and a rewarding place to work for the staff. We have now spread across 4 cities and are only growing.
